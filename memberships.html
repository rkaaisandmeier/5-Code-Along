Membership & Visit Information

We offer flexible membership options to fit your needs, from drop-in day passes to monthly and annual memberships with unlimited access to our tools and workshops.

Day Pass

Perfect for visitors or those who want to try out the space. Includes access to all tools and open lab hours for one day.

Monthly Membership

Ideal for regular makers. Provides unlimited access to the space, discounted workshop rates, and priority registration for special events.

Annual Membership

Best value for dedicated makers. Includes all the benefits of monthly membership plus exclusive perks like free guest passes and merchandise discounts.
